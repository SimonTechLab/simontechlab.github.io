About

Simon

Swiss engineer with a focus on automation, optimization, and smart design. I create focused digital tools — from streamlined websites to applied AI and quantitative systems. Effective by design.

How It Started

I have always been drawn to optimization and automation — the art of making things work better, smarter, and with less friction. That interest led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at ETH Zurich, where I built a strong foundation in structured thinking, problem-solving, and systems design.

From Code to Markets

In my final semesters, I began applying what I’d learned to a new question: how could these skills be used to approach the financial markets in an original, automated, and intelligent way? This marked the beginning of my deep focus on quantitative research and trading system design.

Balancing Logic and Creativity

My work is driven by two core forces: a respect for rigorous, conventional learning — the kind found in universities, books, and proven methods — and a belief in bold, independent innovation. I see lasting progress as a balance between the two: grounded thinking and fearless creativity.
